About Us

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic a lot of people are unable to go to the gyms and workout. There are a ton of youtube videos and it's always difficult to figure out what to watch. That's where we come in!


We will figure out what videos work for you with regards to how much time you have available and what equipment you have available. There are millions of videos online and we will find the right ones for you. We will push to keep you motivated and healthy. We want to be that motivating factor that pushes you to continue to excercise each day and stay fit!


Through your input we query our database and find corresponding videos that meet your needs. Work from home can be difficult and making time to workout can be hard as well. Let us manage that for you and enjoy your workouts. With weekly schedules at the start of each week, you won't ever have to worry about finding the right workout video again!
